Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we ("the Developer") handle information in the MusicKitApp application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Apple Music Account Information: Authentication information required to access your Apple Music library
  • Music Playback History: Songs played, play counts, playback positions, and playback dates
  • Playlist Information: Custom playlists created by users and their contents
  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app operation (such as OS version)

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing app services and features
  • Recording music playback history and displaying statistics
  • Managing custom playlists and smart playlists
  • Improving app quality and resolving issues
  • Providing user support

4. Data Storage

Information collected by the App is stored in the following locations:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored on the user's device
  • iCloud Storage: Data using SwiftData may be synchronized to the user's iCloud account

We do not transmit or store user data on our servers.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not provide personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When the user has given consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect life, body, or property

6. Apple Music Integration

The App uses the Apple MusicKit framework to access your Apple Music library. This integration requires your explicit permission.

For information handling related to Apple Music, please also review Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Deletion

Users can delete data through the following methods:

  • Delete individual playlists or playback history within the app
  • Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data
  • Delete the app's iCloud data from iCloud settings

8. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information. All data is securely stored on the user's device or iCloud account.

9. Children Under 13

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. If a child under 13 uses the App, parental consent is required.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised policy will take effect when posted on this page.

11. Contact

For inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Contact page.
